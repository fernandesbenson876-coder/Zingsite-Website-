❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I don't pay on time?

Per our terms, work pauses after 3 days of delayed payment. Website may be temporarily suspended until payment is received. No penalty for reactivation.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, with 15 days notice. Setup fee is non-refundable after work starts. No further charges after cancellation.

Do I own the website code?

You own your content, but the hosting is part of the monthly retainer. If you cancel, the live site goes offline. You can transfer domain and get a copy of your content.

How many edits per month?

Basic: 3, Standard: 5, Professional: 10 edits/month. Additional edits ₹300 each. Unused edits don't roll over.

How do I get proof of agreement?

After submitting, you'll receive a detailed confirmation message on WhatsApp. Take a screenshot or use WhatsApp's export chat feature to save as PDF.
