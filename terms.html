Terms & Conditions

1. Web Design Services
Zingsite provides website design, hosting, content editing, and maintenance as per selected plan. Delivery within 5–7 working days after receiving all content.
2. Reel Editing Services
You shoot raw footage, we edit including captions and thumbnails. Delivery within 3 working days. Reels are delivered via WhatsApp or Google Drive.
3. Tally Voucher Printing
We print vouchers exactly as they appear in your Tally data. We do not modify accounting entries. Delivery within 1-2 working days after receiving data.
4. Payment Terms
Web setup fees due before work begins. Monthly retainer due by 1st of each month. Work will pause if payment delayed >3 days. Tally printing: full payment before dispatch. Reel editing: monthly advance payment.
5. Content & Data Responsibility
Client is responsible for providing accurate content (text, images, logo) for websites. For Tally printing, client is responsible for data accuracy before sending.
6. Cancellation Policy
Web plans: 15 days notice before renewal. Setup fee non-refundable after work begins. Reel editing: cancel anytime with 7 days notice. Tally printing: no refunds after printing starts.
7. Hosting & Ownership
Websites hosted on Zingsite infrastructure. If retainer cancelled, site goes offline. Content belongs to client. Domain can be transferred.
8. Limitation of Liability
Zingsite is not liable for business loss due to downtime, printing errors from client data, or third-party issues.
9. Confidentiality
We never share your business data, Tally files, or website content with third parties.
10. Portfolio Rights
We may showcase your website and sample work unless you opt out in writing.
