Terms & Conditions

1. Services
Zingsite provides website design, hosting, content editing, and reel editing for small businesses. Specific services delivered are as agreed per plan selected.
2. Payment Terms — All Payments Are Upfront
All fees must be paid in full before work or service begins:
  • Setup fee — paid upfront before project starts, no exceptions
  • Monthly retainer — paid upfront on or before the 1st of each month
  • Semi-annual plan — full 6-month amount paid upfront before work begins
  • Annual plan — full 12-month amount paid upfront before work begins
💳 Accepted payment methods: UPI / GPay / PhonePe — UPI ID: 8828685342
⚠️ Work will pause if payment is delayed beyond 3 days.
3. Refund Policy
Please review carefully before making payment:
  • Setup fee — Non-refundable once work has begun
  • Monthly retainer — Non-refundable. No partial refunds for unused days in the month
  • Semi-annual plan — Non-refundable after 15 days from the plan start date
  • Annual plan — Non-refundable after 30 days (1 month) of service
⚠️ We strongly recommend reviewing all terms before making any payment. Refund requests outside the above windows will not be processed under any circumstances.
4. Content Edits
Basic: 3, Standard: 5, Professional: 10 edits/month. Extra edits charged at ₹300 each. Unused edits do not carry over to the next month. One edit = one change (text, image, or price update).
5. Turnaround Time
New website: 5–7 working days from setup payment and content receipt. Content edits: within 48 hours. Reel delivery: within 3 working days of receiving footage.
6. Client Responsibilities
Provide all content (text, images, logo, contact details) within 3 days of setup payment. Delays in content may delay delivery. All communication via WhatsApp.
7. Hosting & Ownership
Website is hosted on Zingsite's infrastructure as part of the monthly retainer. If the retainer is cancelled, the site goes offline. All client-provided content (text, images, logo) remains the client's property. The domain can be transferred to the client upon request after cancellation.
8. Cancellation Policy
Cancellation requests must be sent via WhatsApp at least 15 days before the next renewal date. Setup fee is non-refundable after work begins. No partial refunds for unused months. Clients who return after cancellation will not be charged the setup fee again.
9. Plan Upgrades
Upgrading from one plan to another requires a one-time upgrade fee: Basic → Standard: ₹2,500 | Basic → Professional: ₹5,000 | Standard → Professional: ₹2,500. New monthly retainer applies from the next billing cycle.
10. Limitation of Liability & Confidentiality
Zingsite is not liable for loss of business, revenue, or data due to downtime or third-party issues. We will never share your business information, content, or contact details with any third party without your consent.
11. Portfolio Rights
Zingsite reserves the right to showcase your website as part of our portfolio unless you request otherwise in writing via WhatsApp.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I don't pay on time?

Work pauses after 3 days of delayed payment. Website may be temporarily suspended until payment is received. No extra penalty for reactivation.

Can I get a refund?

Setup fee: non-refundable once work begins. Monthly: non-refundable. Semi-annual: non-refundable after 15 days from start. Annual: non-refundable after 30 days of service. Please review all terms before paying.

Why are all payments upfront?

Upfront payments ensure we can allocate dedicated time and resources to your project. It also protects both parties — we start work with confidence, and you're committed to the service.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, with 15 days notice before your next renewal date. Setup fee is non-refundable after work starts. No further charges after cancellation is confirmed.

Do I own the website?

You own your content (text, images, logo). Hosting is part of the monthly retainer — if cancelled, the live site goes offline. You can get your content and transfer your domain anytime.

How many edits do I get per month?

Basic: 3, Standard: 5, Professional: 10 edits/month. Extra edits are ₹300 each. Unused edits don't roll over.

How do I get proof of agreement?

After submitting, you'll receive a detailed confirmation on WhatsApp. Take a screenshot or use WhatsApp's export chat feature to save it as PDF proof.
